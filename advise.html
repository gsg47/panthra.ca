Security Built on Strategy

Tools alone don't make you secure. Direction does. The Advise service gives you the strategy and governance to spend wisely and improve continuously: honest assessments, practical compliance, virtual CISO leadership, and a security-aware team.

01

Security Assessments

You can't fix what you can't see. Our assessments and penetration testing identify vulnerabilities and validate your defenses, showing you exactly where you stand against real-world attacks.

What it includes

  • Penetration testing
  • Security posture & gap assessments
  • Cloud & application reviews
  • Prioritized remediation roadmap

The benefits

  • A clear, honest picture of your risk
  • Fixes ranked by real impact
  • Proof of due diligence for stakeholders

Why businesses need it

Assumptions about security are dangerous. Assessments replace guesswork with evidence, revealing the gaps attackers would exploit and giving you a prioritized plan to close them before they're used against you.

02

Compliance Services

Regulations and standards are complex and always changing. We help your business meet its obligations, reducing risk and turning compliance from a burden into a competitive advantage.

What it includes

  • Readiness & gap analysis
  • Policy & control development
  • Audit preparation & support
  • Ongoing compliance management

The benefits

  • Meet regulatory & contractual requirements
  • Win business that requires compliance
  • Avoid fines & reputational damage

Why businesses need it

Non-compliance risks fines, lost contracts, and reputational harm, and many clients now require proof of security before they'll sign. Practical compliance guidance keeps you audit-ready and opens doors instead of closing them.

Simplify your compliance
03

Security Strategy / vCISO

Not every business needs a full-time security executive, but every business needs the leadership. Our virtual CISO service gives you senior strategy and roadmap guidance on demand, without the full-time cost.

What it includes

  • Security strategy & roadmap
  • Risk management & governance
  • Budget & vendor guidance
  • Board & leadership reporting

The benefits

  • Executive-level expertise, flexible cost
  • A clear plan instead of ad-hoc fixes
  • Security aligned to business goals

Why businesses need it

Without senior leadership, security becomes a random collection of tools with no strategy. A vCISO provides the direction, priorities, and accountability of a seasoned executive at a fraction of the cost of a full-time hire.

Explore vCISO services
04

Security Awareness & Training

Your people are your largest attack surface, and your strongest defense. We turn employees into a confident first line of defense with practical training and realistic phishing simulations.

What it includes

  • Security awareness training
  • Phishing simulation campaigns
  • Role-based & onboarding modules
  • Progress tracking & reporting

The benefits

  • Fewer clicks on phishing & scams
  • A security-aware culture
  • Measurable reduction in human risk

Why businesses need it

The vast majority of breaches involve human error. Technology alone can't stop a convincing phishing email. Ongoing awareness training builds the instincts your team needs to recognize and report threats before they cause harm.
