# Reply & Objection Scripts

When someone replies, **stop the sequence**. Answer within a few hours if you can.

---

## Positive / interested

**They say:** "Sure, let's talk" / "Send times" / "What does it cost?"

```
Great — thanks {{FirstName}}.

Two options:
1) Reply with 2–3 windows that work (MT), or
2) Use the contact form and mention "Security Snapshot": https://panthra.ca/contact.html

The Snapshot is complimentary. If you later want a deeper assessment or an ongoing package (Vigil / Phantom), I'll quote clearly before any paid work.

Talk soon,
Gurshaan
```

---

## Soft yes / send more info

```
Happy to. Short version:

• 30 minutes on a call or screenshare
• Focus: identity, email/M365, endpoints, backups
• You get a one-page risk summary afterward
• No obligation

If it looks useful, we can discuss a paid assessment or a Vigil starter package after — never during the Snapshot unless you ask.

Want to pick a time this week?
```

---

## "How much do you charge?"

```
Fair question.

The Security Snapshot itself is free.

Paid work depends on scope:
• One-time Security Assessment — scoped after we see your environment
• Vigil (foundation) — ongoing essential controls for smaller teams
• Phantom — adds 24/7 monitoring / detection & response

I won't guess a number blind. After the Snapshot I can give you a clear quote with what's included and what isn't.

Open to locking a 30-minute slot?
```

---

## "We already have an IT provider"

```
That's common — and usually a good thing.

Most IT providers handle day-to-day systems well. Where we fit is the security layer they don't always cover deeply: identity hardening, phishing defense, monitoring, and incident response.

We're built to partner alongside IT, not replace them.

If useful, the Snapshot can include your IT contact so everyone's aligned.
```

```
Gurshaan
```

Log as **Unsubscribed** in the tracker. Do not email again.

```

For something useful to {{Company}} specifically, the 30-minute Snapshot usually beats a generic PDF — you'll leave with a risk one-pager tied to your setup.
```

---

## No reply after Email 4

Mark **Closed — no reply**. Move on. Do not restart the sequence for 90+ days unless you have a new trigger (breach news in their industry, referral, insurance renewal season).

---

## On the discovery / Snapshot call (agenda)

1. Confirm what systems they use (M365? endpoints? backups?).
2. Ask what worries them most (phishing, ransomware, compliance, insurance).
3. Share 3 plain findings + 1 recommended next step.
4. If fit: propose paid assessment or Vigil with a clear next action and price range.
5. If not fit: say so and offer a referral path or a later check-in.

**Close line:** "If you want help fixing the top issues, I can send a one-page scope and quote by tomorrow."
