## Personalization bank (steal these patterns)

| Signal you found | First-line personalization |
|------------------|----------------------------|
| They advertise remote / hybrid work | "Noticed {{Company}} runs a hybrid team on Microsoft 365…" |
| Law firm / clinic / accounting | "Firms like {{Company}} are high-value targets because of the client data you hold…" |
| Recent growth / hiring | "Saw {{Company}} is growing — new staff and vendors usually expand the attack surface fast…" |
| Cyber insurance mentioned / regulated | "With cyber insurance and compliance pressure rising, a quick posture check tends to pay for itself…" |
| Public email on website is generic | "Reached out because {{Company}}'s client work depends on email trust…" |

Never fake research. If you only have their industry + size, use the industry line and keep it honest.
