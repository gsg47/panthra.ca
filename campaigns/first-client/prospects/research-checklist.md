# Prospect Research Checklist

Goal: build a list of **40–60** real Calgary / Alberta businesses that match the ICP, with a named human and a work email.

## Where to find companies

1. Google Maps / local directories — "law firm Calgary", "dental clinic Calgary", "accounting firm Calgary", "insurance broker Calgary"
2. LinkedIn search — Company size 11–200, location Calgary / Alberta, industries above
3. Chamber / industry association directories (Calgary Chamber, Law Society public directories, CPA Alberta where public)
4. Your network — ask for intros first; warm beats cold
5. Existing website contact pages — many list a general or partner email

## Qualify in (must pass)

- [ ] 15–150 employees (estimate is fine)
- [ ] No obvious large security vendor / in-house CISO branding
- [ ] Uses email heavily / has a professional website
- [ ] Fits a target industry
- [ ] You can name a decision-maker (or a strong gatekeeper)

## Qualify out

- Enterprise / national brands with security careers pages
- Companies that only list a contact form and no humans (harder for first campaign)
- Competitors and MSPs you don't want to pitch yet
- Anyone who previously opted out

## Find the right person

Priority order:

1. Owner / Founder / Managing Partner
2. Operations Manager / Office Manager
3. IT Manager (if small team)
4. General manager

Avoid blasting `info@` alone when a named partner email exists.

## Minimum research before sending (3 minutes)

Capture in the tracker:

| Field | Example |
|-------|---------|
| Company | Riverbend Law LLP |
| Industry | Legal |
| City | Calgary |
| Contact name | Jordan Lee |
| Title | Managing Partner |
| Email | jordan@riverbendlaw.ca |
| Personalization note | Hybrid team, client portal mentioned on site |
| Source | Google Maps + site contact page |

## Email verification (lightweight)

- Prefer addresses published on the company site
- Pattern guess only when confident (`firstname@`, `first.last@`) and verify carefully
- If unsure, use the public contact address and address the email to the role: "Hi Jordan — routing this to you as Managing Partner…"

## Weekly list target

| Day | Output |
|-----|--------|
| Mon | Research 15 prospects |
| Tue | Send Email 1 to 8; research 8 more |
| Wed | Send Email 1 to 8; follow-ups due |
| Thu | Send Email 1 to 8; process replies |
| Fri | Follow-ups + book calls; refill list to 40+ active |

## Do not

- Buy scraped email dumps
- Scrape LinkedIn at scale with bots
- Send the same first line to every prospect
- BCC dozens of people on one email
