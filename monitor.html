Always Watching, So You Don't Have To

The average breach goes undetected for months. The Monitor service closes that gap with continuous visibility across your systems, combining a managed SIEM/SOC, active threat detection, ongoing vulnerability management, and clear reporting so you always know where you stand.

01

Security Monitoring (SIEM/SOC)

We collect and correlate security signals from across your environment in a managed SIEM, watched around the clock by a Security Operations Center. Suspicious activity is investigated and escalated the moment it appears, day or night.

What it includes

  • Managed SIEM setup & log collection
  • 24/7 Security Operations Center coverage
  • Correlation & alert triage by analysts
  • Escalation & response guidance

The benefits

  • Threats are seen in minutes, not months
  • No need to build an in-house SOC
  • Constant coverage on nights & weekends

Why businesses need it

Attacks don't keep business hours. Without continuous monitoring, an intruder can operate undetected for weeks. A managed SIEM/SOC gives you enterprise-grade visibility and expert eyes on your environment at all times.

Start 24/7 monitoring
02

Threat Detection

Beyond simple alerts, we use behavioral analytics and current threat intelligence to hunt for the subtle signs of compromise: unusual logins, lateral movement, and behaviour that doesn't fit.

What it includes

  • Behavioral & anomaly detection
  • Threat intelligence enrichment
  • Proactive threat hunting
  • Detection tuning to cut false alarms

The benefits

  • Catches attacks that bypass basic tools
  • Fewer false alarms, faster real ones
  • Early warning before damage is done

Why businesses need it

Modern attackers blend in with normal activity to avoid detection. Behaviour-based threat detection spots the anomalies that signature-based tools miss, letting you stop an intruder while they're still looking around, not after they've struck.

Improve your threat detection
03

Vulnerability Management

We continuously scan your systems for weaknesses, then help you prioritize and fix the ones that actually matter, so you're patching the holes attackers would use first.

What it includes

  • Continuous vulnerability scanning
  • Risk-based prioritization
  • Remediation tracking & guidance
  • Regular re-testing & verification

The benefits

  • Close the gaps attackers exploit most
  • Spend effort where risk is highest
  • Measurable reduction in exposure over time

Why businesses need it

New vulnerabilities appear every day, and attackers weaponize them fast. A one-time scan goes stale within weeks. Ongoing vulnerability management keeps a live picture of your exposure and ensures the riskiest issues get fixed first.

Get a vulnerability review
04

Security Reporting

Security data is only useful if it drives decisions. We turn activity, alerts, and trends into clear, regular reports your team and leadership can actually understand and act on.

What it includes

  • Executive & technical reporting
  • Trend & incident summaries
  • Compliance-ready evidence
  • Regular review meetings

The benefits

  • Everyone sees where you stand
  • Confident conversations with leadership & boards
  • Ready evidence for audits & insurers

Why businesses need it

You can't improve what you can't measure, and you can't justify security spend without proof of value. Clear reporting gives leadership visibility, supports compliance, and turns your security program into a story you can tell with confidence.
