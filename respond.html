Turn a Crisis Into a Contained Event

When an incident hits, preparation is everything. The Respond service makes sure you're ready, with a rapid incident response capability, tested backups, business continuity planning, and clear emergency playbooks so your team acts decisively instead of scrambling.

01

Incident Response

When something goes wrong, speed matters. Our incident response team moves fast to contain the threat, remove the attacker, and get you back to normal, minimizing damage, downtime, and disruption.

What it includes

  • Rapid triage & containment
  • Threat eradication & recovery
  • Forensic investigation
  • Post-incident hardening

The benefits

  • Less damage & downtime
  • Calm, expert response under pressure
  • Clear answers on what happened

Why businesses need it

In an active attack, every minute counts. Trying to figure out what to do mid-crisis leads to costly mistakes. A ready incident response capability means the moment trouble hits, experienced professionals take control and limit the fallout.

Prepare your incident response
02

Backup & Disaster Recovery

If data is lost or encrypted, reliable, tested backups are what get you back on your feet. We make sure your critical data can be recovered quickly after ransomware, hardware failure, or accidental deletion.

What it includes

  • Automated, secure backups
  • Offsite & immutable copies
  • Tested restore procedures
  • Recovery time & point objectives

The benefits

  • Recover quickly instead of paying ransom
  • Confidence your backups actually work
  • Survive hardware failure & mistakes

Why businesses need it

Ransomware and hardware failures are a matter of when, not if. Many businesses discover too late that their backups were incomplete or untested. Verified backup and disaster recovery turns a potential company-ending event into a recoverable inconvenience.

Protect your backups
03

Business Continuity

Recovery isn't only about data, it's about keeping the business running. We help you plan how essential operations continue through a disruption, so a security event doesn't grind everything to a halt.

What it includes

  • Business impact analysis
  • Continuity & contingency planning
  • Roles, communications & runbooks
  • Plan testing & exercises

The benefits

  • Operations keep moving during a crisis
  • Everyone knows their role
  • Protected revenue & reputation

Why businesses need it

A breach or outage affects far more than IT, it hits sales, service, and customer trust. Without a continuity plan, teams improvise and mistakes multiply. Planning ahead keeps critical functions running while you recover.

Build your continuity plan
04

Emergency Response Planning

The best time to prepare for a crisis is before it happens. We build clear, practical playbooks and escalation paths so your team knows exactly what to do the moment a serious incident strikes.

What it includes

  • Incident playbooks & runbooks
  • Escalation & decision paths
  • Contact trees & communication plans
  • Tabletop exercises & drills

The benefits

  • Fast, coordinated response under pressure
  • No confusion about who does what
  • A tested plan, not a panicked scramble

Why businesses need it

In the chaos of a real incident, undocumented plans fall apart. Predefined emergency response playbooks remove guesswork, so your team reacts quickly and correctly instead of losing precious time deciding what to do.

Create your response plan
