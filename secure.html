A Hardened Foundation for Your Business

Most breaches exploit a control that was never put in place. The Secure service closes those gaps across the four areas attackers target most: who can access your systems, the infrastructure that runs them, the data and devices that hold your information, and the cloud platforms your team uses every day.

Identity & Access Security Infrastructure Security Data & Endpoint Protection Microsoft 365 Security
01

Identity & Access Security

Identity is the new perimeter. We make sure the right people have the right access to the right systems, and that everyone else is kept out, using multi-factor authentication, least-privilege access, and modern identity governance.

What it includes

  • Multi-factor authentication (MFA) rollout
  • Single sign-on & conditional access
  • Least-privilege & role-based access reviews
  • Privileged account & admin protection

The benefits

  • Stops the vast majority of account takeovers
  • Limits the damage a single compromise can cause
  • Cleaner audits and easier compliance

Why businesses need it

Stolen or weak credentials are behind the majority of breaches. Without MFA and access controls, one phished password can hand an attacker the keys to your entire business. Strong identity security is the single highest-impact control most organizations can put in place.

Strengthen your access controls
02

Infrastructure Security

Your devices, servers, firewalls, and networks are the machinery that keeps your business running. We harden that machinery with layered, defense-in-depth controls so a single weak point can't bring everything down.

What it includes

  • Firewall & network segmentation
  • Server & system hardening
  • Secure configuration & patch management
  • Remote access & VPN protection

The benefits

  • Fewer exploitable entry points
  • Attacks are contained instead of spreading
  • More reliable, resilient operations

Why businesses need it

Unpatched servers, flat networks, and default configurations are exactly what attackers scan for. Hardened infrastructure means that even if one device is compromised, the attacker can't move freely across your environment, turning a potential disaster into a contained incident.

Harden your infrastructure
03

Data & Endpoint Protection

Your data is your most valuable asset, and every laptop and phone is a doorway to it. We protect both, encrypting sensitive information and defending every endpoint with modern detection and data-loss prevention.

What it includes

  • Endpoint detection & response (EDR)
  • Device & disk encryption
  • Data-loss prevention (DLP)
  • Mobile device & USB controls

The benefits

  • Sensitive data stays protected, even if a device is lost
  • Malware is stopped at the device level
  • Confidential information can't quietly walk out the door

Why businesses need it

Remote and hybrid work means company data now lives on dozens of devices outside the office. A single lost laptop or infected endpoint can expose customer records and trade secrets. Protecting data and endpoints keeps your information safe wherever your people work.

Protect your data & devices
04

Microsoft 365 Security

Microsoft 365 runs your email, files, and collaboration, which makes it a top target. We secure it properly, hardening identities, email, and cloud apps so day-to-day work stays productive and safe.

What it includes

  • Email security & anti-phishing hardening
  • Secure identity & conditional access setup
  • SharePoint, Teams & OneDrive protection
  • Secure Score review & best-practice tuning

The benefits

  • Far fewer phishing and account-takeover incidents
  • Safe collaboration for remote and hybrid teams
  • You get full value from the security you already pay for

Why businesses need it

Most organizations run Microsoft 365 with default settings that leave the door open to phishing and business email compromise. Proper configuration turns the platform you already own into a genuinely secure workplace, without buying anything new.

Secure your Microsoft 365
